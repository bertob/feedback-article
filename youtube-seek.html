Seeking on Youtube

Changing the current timestamp in a video by clicking on the timeline

Environment

What can I do?

User Action

Hovering on the timeline and then left-clicking

System Action

Current timestamp of the video changes

How do I do it?

Action Presentation

Timeline moving while video is playing indicates that moving it will change the video timestamp

Before

What is going to happen?

Preview

Active

Hovering over the timeline shows a thumbnail, previewing the video at the specified timestamp

During

Is my input working?

Input Feedback

No feedback on whether the input was received

What is happening?

No Action Feedback

Hard cut

After

What happened?

Response

A different part of the video is playing

How do I go back?

Reversibility

Inverse Action

Clicking at the previous timeline position will go back to the previous timestamp
