Pinch to Zoom

Moving two fingers closer together zooms out, moving them apart zooms back in

Environment

What can i do?
How do I do it?

User Action

Swiping on the screen with two fingers

System Action

Zoom level changes according to the relative proximity of the fingers

No Action Presentation

Not advertised anywhere in the interface

Before

What is going to happen?

No Preview

No way to know what will happen without triggering the action

During

What is happening now?

Feedback

Continuously gives information on current progress of the action

User-Controlled

User controls time during the action

Direct Manipulation

Interaction happens directly on the zooming surface

Indirect Manipulation

Scrollbars are indirectly manipulated, reflecting the zooming of the surface

Autonomous

Time is automatic

Interactive

Parts of the behavior, e.g. kinetic scrolling and rubber-band effects, are autonomous movements that happen while the user is not interacting; they can be changed or stopped by the user

After

what happened?
How do I go back?

Feedback

The zoom level has changed

Reversibility

Effects of the action can be reversed with little effort

Inverse Action

Pinching the opposite way will zoom back to the original setting
