Zoom Buttons

Zooming a surface with + and - buttons

Environment

What can i do?
How do I do it?

User Action

Moving the mouse cursor, pressing the left mouse button

System Action

Zoom level increases and decreases in fixed increments, depending on which button is clicked

Action Presentation

The buttons look like all other system buttons, indicating their clickability

Before

What is going to happen?

Preview

Gives information on the results of the action, before the action itself is started

Passive

The "+" and "-" labels on the buttons explain which one increases and which decreases the zoom level

During

What is happening now?

Feedback

Continuously gives information on current progress of the action

Autonomous

Time is automatic

Non-Interactive

Zoom animation

After

what happened?
How do I go back?

Feedback

Zoom level has changed

Reversibility

Effects of the action can be reversed with little effort

Unrelated Action

Pressing the other button will return the zoom level to its previous value
