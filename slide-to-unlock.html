Slide to Unlock

Swiping horizontally on the lockscreen unlocks the device

Environment

What can i do?
How do I do it?

User Action

Swiping the screen horizontally for a certain distance

System Action

Passcode screen opens

Action Presentation

"slide to unlock" appears after a few seconds of idleness; arrow and animation indicate the direction of the gesture

Before

What is going to happen?

Preview

Gives information on the results of the action, before the action itself is started

Passive

Label explains that the gesture will "unlock" the device

Active

When touching the screen without swiping, it briefly slides to the left and then goes back

During

What is happening now?

Feedback

Continuously gives information on current progress of the action

User-Controlled

User controls time

Direct Manipulation

Lock screen directly follows the finger movement

Autonomous

Time is automatic

Interactive

If the user swipes a certain distance and then releases, the action will be automatically completed

After

what happened?
How do I go back?

Feedback

Passcode screen is visible

Reversibility

Effects of the action can be reversed with little effort

Inverse Action

Swiping back to the right will lock the screen again
