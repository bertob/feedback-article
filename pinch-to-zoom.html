Pinch to Zoom

Pinching with two fingers on the screen zooms in and out

Environment

What can I do?

User Action

Swiping on the screen with two fingers

System Action

Zoom level changes depending on the relative proximity of the fingers

How do I do it?

No Action Presentation

Not advertised anywhere in the interface

Before

What is going to happen?

No Preview

No way to know what will happen without triggering the action

During

Is my input working?

No Input Feedback

No information confirming input

What is happening?

Action Feedback

User-Controlled

Direct Manipulation

Interaction happens directly on the zooming surface

After

What happened?

Response

Zoom level has changed

How do I go back?

Reversibility

Inverse Action

Pinching the opposite way zooms back to the original zoom level
